☢️ The Nuclear Excuse: Truth or Pretext?

"If I hadn't terminated the 'horrific nuclear deal' made by President Obama, Iran would have a nuclear bomb 3 years ago." — Donald Trump, Truth Social (2026)

📋 The Verified Reality

  • ✅ IAEA verified Iran was in compliance with JCPOA commitments (2015-2018)
  • ✅ When Trump withdrew US from the deal (May 2018), Iran was still in compliance
  • ⚠️ Iran stopped compliance AFTER US withdrawal (2019 onwards)
  • ⚠️ IAEA reported inspections confirmed compliance until 2021
🚨 CONCLUSION: The nuclear excuse is partially false. Iran was not close to a bomb when Trump broke the deal.
📚 Technical Terms Explained:
  • IAEA/OIEA (International Atomic Energy Agency): UN agency founded in 1957 to promote peaceful nuclear energy and prevent nuclear weapons proliferation. Based in Vienna, Austria.
  • JCPOA (Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action): 2015 nuclear agreement between Iran and P5+1 (US, UK, France, Russia, China, Germany) limiting Iran's nuclear program in exchange for sanctions relief.
